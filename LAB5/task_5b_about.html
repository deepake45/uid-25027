About This Platform

This website has been created to provide a clear and simple introduction to the fundamentals of web development. It focuses on explaining how modern websites are designed, structured, and made interactive.

Our Objective

Our goal is to help beginners understand the core building blocks of the web in an easy and structured manner. Each section highlights the purpose and importance of the technologies used in website creation.

By learning these concepts, you gain the foundation required to start building your own web pages and applications with confidence.
